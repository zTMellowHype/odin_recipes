Deviled Eggs recipe

Deviled-eggs

Eggs

Mayo

Seasoning

Ingredients

  • 6 Eggs
  • 1/4 cup Mayo
  • 1 teaspoon white vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon yellow mustard
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Smoked spanish paprika, for garnish

Steps to make de eggs

  1. Boil de eggs in sauce pan with water and high heat
  2. Crack de egg shells and peel under cool running water, cut peeled eggs in half, remove yolk put yolk in seperate bowl, mash the yolks, add mayo, vinegar, mustard, salt, and pepper, mix.
  3. put mashed yolk mix into the egg white then garnish with paprika and serve
