Baked Chicken

Chicken Thighs

mustard, Salt & Pepper Honey, Red pepper flakes, garlic, olive oil

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Salt and pepper
  • Olive oil
  • Mustard
  • Garlic
  • Red pepper flakes
  1. Preheat de oven: to 425 degrees
  2. Prepare Chicken: In a bowl or on a cutting board generously season both sides of the chicken thighs with salt and pepper; place into a 9x13 baking dish
  3. Prepare de sauce:In another bowl, whisk together the olive oil, both mustards, honey, garlic, and red pepper flakes until well incorporated. Brush sauce over both sides of the chicken thighs.
  4. Bake:Bake for 35 minutes or until the chicken thighs have an internal temperature of 165 F degrees and skin is crispy.
  5. Serve dat
